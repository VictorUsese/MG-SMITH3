Plumbing and Heating Specialists Covering Essex and London Areas

MG SMITH was founded by two engineers who had a dream to change the way that the plumbing and heating industry was represented. We wanted to build a company based on professionalism, great service and fantastic workmanship. We take pride in our brand and value our customers immensely.

Our aim is to provide great service at an affordable price for all our customers.

At MG SMITH we are here with you every step of the way. Whether you need a boiler replacement, renewable energy installation, or heating services, our specialist team of heating engineers and plumbers will go above and beyond to provide you with the best service.

Fully Trained Gas Safe Registered Engineers

For your own peace of mind, we are Gas Safe and OFTEC Registered, and vetted by Checkatrade. Our fully trained heating and plumbing specialists will take care of all your heating and plumbing needs. For any plumbing issues, or heating concerns, or if you’re simply looking to upgrade your current boiler, our team of specialists is here to help. At MG SMITH we are proud to offer our services 24/7.

High Standards. Quality Work

Our standards are high, and we always aim for the sky. Ensuring you are 100% satisfied with what we do is paramount. Our customers at the heart of everything we do here at MG SMITH, which is why our friendly technicians will go above and beyond for you, day in day out. We are most notably known for our quality work, efficiency and top-notch service. Because of this we are the most trusted heating and plumbing specialist in the Surrey Heath area.

We Work Throughout Essex and London

With the MG SMITH Heating office based in Camberley, Surrey Heath we are able to offer our services throughout the South East including Frimley, Lightwater, Bracknell, Woking and Guildford. Our plumbing specialists and heating engineers are also available on hand to residents and business owners in surrounding areas of Essex and London. We cover up to a large radius of Camberley so please get in touch to see if we cover your area.
