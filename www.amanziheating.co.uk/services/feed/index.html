]]> Underfloor Heating & Renewable Energy System Installations
Covering Surrey & Hampshire

Amanzi are passionate about renewable energy and creating systems in homes that are more energy efficient and sustainable. We work closely with manufactures and designers to create a highly specified system tailored to your home and personal needs. Whether in a new build or a refurb we will integrate your home with a renewable energy system.

Reasons to go renewable

  • Savings on energy bills
  • Reduce carbon footprint
  • Better performance
  • Reduce fossil fuel usage.

Underfloor heating

  • 25% more efficient than a traditional system
  • “Hidden heating” the pipes are laid below the screed so you don’t have to worry about bulky radiators taking up space
  • Better heat coverage. The whole room will heat up evenly. This creates a really cozy comfortable atmosphere in your home
  • Individual control of each room
  • Very efficient with a heat pump.

What we offer

  • Underfloor heating supply and fit
  • Servicing systems
  • Rain water harvesting
  • Air source heat pumps
  • Ground source heat pumps
  • Solar thermal
  • Solar PV
  • Biomass boilers

Having a new boiler installed or replaced can be expensive and there are lots of considerations to take into account. Our expert engineers will help you choose the right type of boiler or cylinder for your home that is in line with your budget. With a variety of options available we can advise you on which boiler is best suited to your needs. Our installation and replacements services are available to homes across Surrey and Hampshire, so get in touch with us on 01276 537 213 to get a free quote.

Boilers

Whether your current boiler needs replacing, or you are looking for a new and more efficient boiler for your property then we can help. Our engineers are well experienced in specifying and installing boilers. Our engineers have regular training on a variety of products with manufacturers so not only are you getting a new boiler but it’s being installed by a highly skilled, manufacturer trained engineer.

Why get a new boiler?

  • System specification and sizing
  • New boilers are highly efficient
  • Saving of up to 30% on heating bills
  • Better for the environment
  • Less expense on costly break downs

Cylinders

Amanzi offer a large range of new cylinders supplied and fitted. Whether your old gravity fed cylinder needs replacing or you’d like to get rid of those noisy shower pumps and install a more efficient pressurised cylinder. We’ve got you covered.

Why get a new cylinder?

  • System specification and sizing
  • Cylinder replacements
  • Gravity to pressurised conversions
  • Cylinder relocations
  • Boosted pressure systems for the whole house
